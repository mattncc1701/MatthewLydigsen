Overview



This video is of my FRC team back in 2013 at the Boston State Regional Finals. Our team one the regional that year as well as the Chairman's award. Since sophomore year of high school i have been heavily involved in FIRST. FIRST allows high school students working along with adult mentors to build 120 pound robots to accomplish challenges FIRST presents every year. The challenge in the video above is shooting frisbees into goals and climbing the pyramids seen in the video. FIRST provides very challenging technical challenges every year and it is because of FIRST that I got my extensive background in programming, embedded systems, sensors, electronics, and overall love for engineering. If you want to learn more about FIRST visit their website by clicking on the FIRST Logo in the left column.

Promoting STEM



Above is picture of my friends and I with U.S. Representative Niki Tsongas. We met with her to talk about spreading STEM throughout the community. STEM stands for Science Technology Engineering and Mathemtics and these fields of study are severly lacking in our public school system. FIRST is all about promoting STEM throughout the world and as a member of FIRST it is also my responsiblity. Teaching the youth about these fields will be immensly important in the future since there are more jobs opening in those fields every day and fewer people to take them.
