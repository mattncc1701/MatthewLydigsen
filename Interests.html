Graphics, Vision, and Robotics

If you have seen my projects page you can tell I have done a lot of graphics work. My sub tract at Umass is Graphics, Vision, and Robotics so I take classes specific towards those subjects. I would say there are two major appeals to these fields of study for me. For one they give me a chance to diversify my study so that I can learn about multiple disciplines. In addition, when I write a program for Graphics or Robotics I actually see the results. What I mean by this is that instead of just seeing some lines of text appear I see an image I made appear or I see a robot move and do something spectacular because I programmed it to.
